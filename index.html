Introduction on myself

My name is Nischal Bhandari and welcome to my website. I am a first-year student at the University of Technology Sydney studying a Bachelor of Information Technology. As an exchange student from Nepal, I am fluent in multiple languages like Nepali being my mother tounge, English and Hindi. I am very reserved person and appreciate small crowds with my close ones. I always had a dream of studying Information Technology in abroad, so I was glad when UTS took me in.
